See your site for each change

Proviso creates a persistent environment where you can see what your what your site will look like after every change. We keep the environment around as long as you want it so that your developers, QA team, project managers, and even clients can see your work in progress (if you want them to, that is).

Integrated with Github and Stash

We live in our pull request and our bet is that you probably do too. Proviso integrates with your pull requests sending the status back so that you can get links to your environment and your test results right from inside your pull requests.

Configured with a simple YAML file inside your repository

Just log into create a .proviso.yml yaml file that tells us how to setup your site and how to run your tests.

Optimized for Data

We will build an environment from your sanitized database backups so that you can see what your site is really going to look like with real content and the the most recent configuration.
